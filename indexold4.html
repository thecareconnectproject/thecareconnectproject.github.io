About Me

Hi! I'm Julie, an upcoming freshman at Lassiter. I have varied hobbies and interests, including colorguard, theatre, and reading.

My goal for this project is to earn enough money to go to Europe in a couple of years for marching band. I plan to continue this project throughout my freshman year as well.

I take all honors/advanced classes and will be joining Lassiter's STEM Academy this fall, so tutoring younger students is a natural fit — and as a dog owner, dog care comes easily to me too.

Every dollar earned goes toward that Europe trip. Thank you for supporting a student-run business!
